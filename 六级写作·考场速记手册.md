## 一、三大反模式骨架

### 骨架A：现象评论类（考频最高！）

**Para 1 引入（2句）**
> ① **The past decade has witnessed a growing emphasis on ______.**
> ② **This shift is particularly relevant for ______ in today's ______.**

**Para 2 论证（3句）**
> ③ **What makes ______ particularly compelling is its capacity to ______.**
> ④ **For instance, ______.**
> ⑤ **Beyond that, ______ also plays a vital role in ______.**

**Para 3 辩证收尾（2句）**
> ⑥ **Of course, it would be naive to overlook the challenges — ______.**
> ⑦ **Nevertheless, ______ is a direction worth pursuing.**

---

### 骨架B：正反观点类

**Para 1 引出争议（2句）**
> ① **There is no shortage of debate over whether ______.**
> ② **Some view it as a step forward, while others remain skeptical.**

**Para 2 正反各2句**
> ③ **Those in favor argue that ______. For many, this means ______.**
> ④ **On the other hand, critics point out that ______. They worry that ______, and rightly so.**

**Para 3 我的立场（2句）**
> ⑤ **Personally, I find myself leaning toward the former / latter camp.**
> ⑥ **The key, to my mind, is to strike a sensible balance between ______ and ______.**

---

### 骨架C：解决问题类

**Para 1 问题陈述（2句）**
> ① **In recent years, ______ has become an increasingly pressing concern.**
> ② **Failing to address it could lead to far-reaching consequences.**

**Para 2 方案（3句）**
> ③ **One effective approach is to ______.**
> ④ **At the same time, ______ should also take steps to ______.**
> ⑤ **If these measures are implemented, we can expect to see meaningful improvement.**

**Para 3 总结（1-2句）**
> ⑥ **All in all, tackling ______ requires collective effort rather than individual action alone.**
> ⑦ **The sooner we act, the better positioned we will be to create a more ______ future.**

## 二、万能过渡句（记住3个）

| 功能 | 句子 |
|:---:|:---|
| 引出例子 | **A case in point is that...** |
| 让步转折 | **Admittedly, it is true that... Yet this does not change the fact that...** |
| 总结升华 | **The significance of ______, therefore, cannot be overstated.** |

## 三、10个必用高级替换

| 低分 | → | 高分 |
|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| important | → | **crucial / vital / of great significance** |
| more and more | → | **an increasing number of / increasingly** |
| because | → | **due to / owing to** |
| should | → | **be supposed to / it is advisable to** |
| many | → | **numerous / a multitude of** |
| good | → | **beneficial / favorable** |
| bad | → | **detrimental / adverse** |
| think | → | **contend / assert / reckon** |
| very | → | **exceedingly / remarkably** |
| help | → | **facilitate / contribute to** |

## 四、范文1：数字素养 ✍️

> **题目：** importance of developing digital literacy among college students

The past decade has witnessed a growing emphasis on digital literacy, and this shift is particularly relevant for college students in today's digital age.

What makes digital literacy particularly compelling is its capacity to help us distinguish reliable information from misinformation and prepare us for future careers. For instance, students who strengthen their digital skills are better equipped to identify fake news and protect their privacy online. Beyond that, digital literacy also plays a vital role in safeguarding personal data and avoiding online scams.

Of course, it would be naive to overlook the challenges — not everyone has equal access to digital resources. Nevertheless, investing in digital literacy is a direction worth pursuing for every college student.

## 五、范文2：心理健康 ✍️

> **题目：** how college students can maintain mental well-being in a competitive environment

The past decade has witnessed a growing emphasis on mental well-being, and this shift is particularly relevant for college students in today's competitive environment.

What makes mental well-being particularly important is its capacity to help us stay focused and motivated. For instance, students who make it a point to enjoy each day are better able to relax themselves. Beyond that, maintaining mental health also plays a vital role in sustaining academic performance and building healthy relationships.

Of course, it would be naive to overlook the challenges — not only is academic pressure intense, but competition is also fierce. Nevertheless, doing one's job while maintaining a balanced life is a direction worth pursuing.

## 六、范文3：终身学习 ✍️

> **题目：** the importance of lifelong learning for career development

The past decade has witnessed a growing emphasis on lifelong learning, and this shift is particularly relevant for young professionals in today's rapidly changing job market.

What makes lifelong learning particularly compelling is its capacity to keep our skills relevant and open up new career opportunities. For instance, those who continuously update their knowledge are better able to adapt to industry shifts and technological advances. Beyond that, lifelong learning also plays a vital role in fostering creativity and maintaining intellectual curiosity.

Of course, it would be naive to overlook the challenges — finding time for learning while working full-time is not easy. Nevertheless, investing in continuous learning is a direction worth pursuing for anyone seeking long-term career growth.

## 七、考场顺序备忘 ⏱

| 时间 | 动作 |
|:---:|:---|
| 开考2分钟 | **确定题型**（现象/正反/解决问题）→ 选骨架 |
| 5分钟 | **关键词列提纲**（中文就行） |
| 20分钟 | **写！** 用骨架往里填，别纠结用词 |
| 3分钟 | **检查**：主谓一致 + 时态 + 拼写 |
| 最后 | **字数够150词就行**，别贪多 |
